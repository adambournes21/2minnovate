Who We Are

Michael Mattarock

Michael Mattarock is a professor at CMU's INI, former director of national security research at CMU, and has served in the U.S. State Department and the Air Force. With decades of expertise in national security and advanced research, he guides organizations in leveraging emerging technologies.
